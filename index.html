WELCOME TO TEXT ADVENTURE!(BETA)

This is a text based adventure game.

The game works like a normal text game, there's a picture in some situations and then there are choices and buttons for you to press. There will also be other inputs you will be required to type, like your name or the item you want to use or get. It all depends on your current situation. For now, you need to type your name so you can start the adventure.
